About us

THE STORY OF ACADEMIA Y FALAKROS

Academia y Falakros started out as a dream, born virtually out of its founder’s head, Dr. Jonathan Smith, in 1920 with the help of a reputed group of educators from the Department of Economics in University of Michigan.

OUR VISION

Guided by the core values of Fortitude, Excellence, and Uprightness, Academia y Falakros aims to be a university of choice in Asia

OUR MISSION

Academia y Falakros provides quality higher education through industry-responsive and outcomes-based curricular programs.

AF produces globally competitive graduates who exhibit the core values of fortitude, excellence, and uprightness.

AF promotes sustainable and responsive research, extension, heritage and environment stewardship towards national and global development.

CORE VALUES

AF graduates are globally competitive and exhibit the core values of fortitude, excellence, and uprightness.
